Solar Panel & Pollution

Pollutions damages the environment, like water and plants, and also the health of humans. A way to stop pollution is going solar energy. Solar energy created by solar panels, can help stop air pollution around the world. Solar panels will reduce global warming, improve the health of human beings, and save consumers money.
